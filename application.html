Ultrasonic Industrial Cleaning and Degreasing

Essential for metal surface finish industries viz. electroplating, lacquering, vacuum-metallizing, anodizing, machining, tooling process, powder-coating, etc.

Thoroughclean offers an environment-friendly, pollution-free, economical, faster, reliable, and user-friendly ultrasonic cleaning machine in various configurations ranging from one liter to thousand liter capacity for numerous applications.

Prior to metal surface coating/plating, it is essential that the surface should be totally free from dirt, grease, oil, and oxide. Otherwise, plating or coating may peel off, chip, or degrade due to poor adhesion. Ultrasonic cleaning ensures removal of deeply embedded dirt and foreign impurities from crevices or blind holes which are normally difficult to reach.

Conventional degreasing methods using toxic chemicals can be completely avoided. No more brushing, dipping, or rubbing required. Ultrasonic cleaning is fast, consistent, efficient, non-toxic, and environmentally safe.

It works efficiently on substrates such as Aluminum, Zinc, Brass, Copper, Iron, Stainless Steel, and others. Ultrasonic cleaning ensures high-quality surface finish and longer-lasting coating performance.

Ultrasonic Cleaning SAVES:

  • Process time
  • Manpower
  • Energy
  • Use of polluting chemicals
  • Cleaning cost
  • Water usage

Metal surface cleanliness level is measurable and reproducible. It helps in removing grease, oils, dust, and oxide from bare metal surfaces, preparing them for coating or further processing.

Be sure, Thoroughclean’s ultrasonic cleaning machine will yield profit, quality awareness, proper cleanliness, and fast payback. Once installed at your workplace, it resolves all cleaning challenges with ease.
